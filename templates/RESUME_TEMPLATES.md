## Formatting Guidelines (All Templates)

### Fonts
- **Sans-serif** (modern, tech): Arial, Calibri, Helvetica
- **Serif** (traditional, business): Times New Roman, Georgia, Garamond
- **Size**: 10-12pt body, 14-18pt name, 11-14pt section headers

### Margins
- Standard: 0.7-1.0 inch all sides
- Tight (for space): 0.5-0.7 inch

### Spacing
- Line spacing: Single (1.0) or tight (0.9-1.0)
- Space between sections: 6-12pt
- Space between bullets: 3-6pt

### Visual Elements
- **Lines**: Simple horizontal lines (─── or ═══) for separation
- **Bullets**: Standard (•, -, *) - choose one and be consistent
- **Bold**: Section headers, company names, job titles
- **Italics**: Dates, locations (sparingly)

### Color (Use Sparingly)
- **ATS-safe**: Stick to black text on white background
- **For human review**: Dark gray (#333) for body, darker for headers
- **Accent color**: One professional color (navy, dark green) for name/headers only

## ATS Compatibility Checklist

All templates must:
- [ ] Use standard section headers
- [ ] Have no tables, text boxes, headers/footers
- [ ] Use standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
- [ ] Be saved as .docx or text-based PDF
- [ ] Have contact info in document body (not header)
- [ ] Use simple bullet points
- [ ] Have consistent date formatting
- [ ] Include no images or graphics

---

## Customization Tips

1. **Choose template based on**:
   - Industry standards (tech vs finance vs creative)
   - Your experience level (entry vs senior)
   - Company culture (startup vs corporate)

2. **Customize colors** (if not ATS-only):
   - Match company brand colors subtly
   - Keep it professional (no neon, no excessive color)

3. **Adjust length**:
   - <10 years experience: 1 page strongly preferred
   - 10-15 years: 1-2 pages acceptable
   - 15+ or executive: 2 pages acceptable

4. **Tailor content**:
   - Mirror job description language
   - Prioritize relevant experiences
   - Adjust skill emphasis

---

## File Naming Convention

**Format**: `FirstName_LastName_Title_Company.pdf`

**Examples**:
- `John_Doe_Software_Engineer_Google.pdf`
- `Jane_Smith_Resume.pdf`
- `Alex_Johnson_Product_Manager_Amazon.pdf`

**Avoid**:
- `Resume.pdf` (too generic)
- `MyResume_Final_v3.pdf` (unprofessional)
- `JD_resume_updated.pdf` (unclear)
